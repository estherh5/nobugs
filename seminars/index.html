Our Recertification Seminars

NoBugs! is on its 33rd year of hosting annual pesticide recertification seminars and webinars approved by the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection.

NoBugs! will continue to host WEBINARS instead of onsite seminars. You must preregister to attend the webinars by calling us at (215) 592-7997 or emailing us at nobugspestcontrol@gmail.com, and there is a $15.00 fee per program (credit/debit cards can be processed over the phone). You should receive all the recertification credits that you need before you renew your license.

You can call or email us with any questions or concerns about your credit needs.
