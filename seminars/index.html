Our Recertification Seminars

NoBugs! is on its 29th year of hosting annual pesticide recertification seminars approved by the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection. All seminars are held at 545 North Orianna Street in Philadelphia, PA from 4:00 to 8:00 PM, with dinner and desserts provided. Each seminar costs $25.00 per person.


Space is limited, so please call Bruce and Linda at (215) 592-7997 or email nobugspestcontrol@gmail.com to make your reservation as soon as possible.
