Our Recertification Seminars

NoBugs! is on its 30th year of hosting annual pesticide recertification seminars approved by the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection.

Due to COVID-19 restrictions, we will not be having our onsite seminars in September. We will be hosting WEBINARS instead.

You must preregister to attend the webinars by calling us at (215) 592-7997 or emailing us at nobugspestcontrol@gmail.com, and there is a $10.00 fee per program (credit/debit cards can be processed over the phone). You must receive all the recertification credits that you need BEFORE you can renew your license. Our September schedule is listed below.

You can call or email us with any questions or concerns about your credit needs.
