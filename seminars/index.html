Our Recertification Seminars

NoBugs! completed its 31st year of hosting annual pesticide recertification seminars approved by the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection.

We will continue hosting webinars for our programs in 2022. We will update the dates, outlines and credits here as we have them. Enjoy the upcoming holidays and have a great new year!

You can call or email us with any questions or concerns about your credit needs.
